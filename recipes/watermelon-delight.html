Watermelon Delight

A cold watermelon drink that's perfect for hot days!

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 5 mins
  • Servings: 2
  • Yield: 2 Servings

ingredients

  • 2 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • 5 ice cubes
  • 2 tablespoon honey

Directions

  1. Step 1: Blend watermelon, ice cubes, and honey in a blender until smooth.
